Make your own

Build a brainrot bot that's all yours.

Brainrot Bot is just the start. Build your own from scratch: give it a name, a backstory, and its own way of talking, then chat with it like any other. Free comes with one custom bot; paid plans hold from 10 up to 100 and let you switch between them anytime. Every bot passes a safety check before it goes live.

Give it a personality

Name it, write who it is, and set the vibe: sweet, deadpan, or completely unhinged.

Teach it to talk

Pick its humor, slang, and go-to emojis, then drop in an example reply so it nails the voice from message one.

Pick its look

Upload an avatar or generate one from a quick description, write its one-line tagline, and save. It lands in your bot picker, ready to go.

Memes, GIFs & stickers

When words fail, send the meme.

Mid-chat, pull up a strip of trending memes and GIFs or search for the exact reaction, then drop it straight into the convo. Stickers work the same way: find one and send it. Some moments deserve a face, not a paragraph.
