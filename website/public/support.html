Support

Need a hand with Meme Chat AI? Email memechataisupport@gmail.com and we'll get back to you. Include your device model, OS version, and a short description of what you're seeing — that's usually enough to track anything down.

Common requests

  • Delete my account. Open the App, go to Settings → Delete all data. Your account, profile on our servers, and every preference on your device are wiped immediately.
  • Change appearance or language. Settings → Appearance / Settings → Language.
  • Forgot your password. On the sign-in screen, tap the email field, then use the password reset link sent by Meme Chat AI to set a new one.
